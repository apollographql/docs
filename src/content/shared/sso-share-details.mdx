1. Go to [GraphOS Studio](https://studio.apollographql.com?referrer=docs-content). Open the **Settings** page from the top navigation. Open the **Org management** tab from the left sidebar and click **Enable SSO** or **Reconfigure SSO** if you are migrating from the legacy setup. A setup wizard appears.
2. Enter the **Email domain**(s) you are setting SSO up for. Click **Continue**.
3. Select **SAML** as the setup type. Click **Continue**.
