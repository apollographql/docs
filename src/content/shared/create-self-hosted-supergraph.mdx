1. Go to your organization's **Graphs** tab in [GraphOS Studio](https://studio.apollographql.com/?referrer=docs-content).

2. Click **Create New Graph** in the top right. Studio displays the following dialog:

<img
  className="screenshot"
  src="../graphos/img/graph-creation.jpg"
  width="350"
  style="margin-bottom: 20px;"
/>

3. Specify an **Organization** and **Graph title** for your graph.

4. Leave the **Graph Architecture** as **Supergraph (Default)**.

5. Click **Next**. A modal like the following appears:

<img
  src="../graphos/img/register-schema-dialog.jpg"
  alt="Schema registration options"
  width="500"
/>

6. Leave the **Supergraph Pipeline Track** dropdown set to its default value.

Your graph has been created!
